# Gaps Register — World-Cup Underdog Draw-No-Bet Backtest

Completeness-critic artifact. Project: [worldcup-underdog-dnb-backtest](../../README.md). Author dimension: SKIE. Date: 2026-06-16.

## Purpose and method

This register is the output of a **completeness critic** pass over the six research pillars — [CALC](research_dnb-odds-calculations_2026-06-16.md), [DATA](research_data-availability_2026-06-16.md), [STAT](research_statistical-methodology_2026-06-16.md), [STAKE](research_staking-bankroll_2026-06-16.md), [EDGE](research_edge-flb-empirics_2026-06-16.md), [ARCH](research_backtest-architecture-deliverables_2026-06-16.md) — plus [methodology.md](../protocol/methodology.md) and the [phased work plan](../protocol/plan_phased-workplan_2026-06-16.md). It records what is **missing or under-specified** for a defensible, reproducible deliverable that meets the project standards (peer-reviewed/official-doc citation at every factual claim; no arbitrary thresholds; methods verified against primary sources; full backtest reporting per [rules/quant-project.md](../../../.claude/rules/quant-project.md)).

Scope discipline (so the register adds signal, not noise):

- Each existing doc already carries an "Open questions" section. Items already raised there are **not** re-listed unless (a) the gap is cross-cutting and **no document owns it**, (b) the open question is under-specified to the point of being unactionable, or (c) a primary-source check during this pass changed the picture. Where a gap restates an existing open question, the owning doc/OQ is named and the *added* requirement is stated.
- Verification performed this pass: 2026 format confirmed (48 teams, 104 matches, 12 groups, **8 best third-placed finishers → a Round of 32**; [Britannica](https://www.britannica.com/event/2026-FIFA-World-Cup), [FIFA](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026), [Al Jazeera 2023-03-14](https://www.aljazeera.com/sports/2023/3/14/fifa-confirms-expanded-2026-world-cup-with-record-104-matches)); [football-data.co.uk/notes.txt](https://www.football-data.co.uk/notes.txt) confirms `PSH/PSD/PSA`, the closing-`C` convention, `PSCH/PSCD/PSCA`, `AHh`, `PAHH/PAHA`, but does **not** contain the 2025 Pinnacle-degradation notice (the docs attribute that to data.php — unverified this pass) and does **not** explicitly enumerate a closing-Pinnacle-AH code, leaving `PCAHH/PCAHA` vs the convention-derived `PAHCH/PAHCA` genuinely ambiguous.

Priority key: **P1** = blocks a defensible/reproducible deliverable or can invert the headline verdict; **P2** = materially affects correctness, reproducibility, or a mandated deliverable item but does not invert the verdict; **P3** = robustness, polish, or documentation completeness.

## Cross-cutting observations (not table rows)

1. **The corpus is unusually complete on theory and unusually thin on execution.** Every probabilistic identity, inference estimator, and selection-bias control is derived and primary-source-verified to a high standard. The genuine gaps cluster in **(a) execution/microstructure** (transaction costs, leg-out, capacity, the actual tradable line) and **(b) the 2026-specific structure** (Round-of-32 qualification, the reference-regime confound, third-place dead-rubber tagging) — i.e. the places where the idealized synthetic instrument meets a real, post-2025-cutover, 48-team tournament. The two P1 microstructure/format gaps (transaction-cost model; 2026 qualification tagging) and the two P1 data-provenance gaps (Pinnacle-notice verification; nothing else can invert the verdict the way a mis-transcribed reference-cutover can) are the load-bearing ones.

2. **Several gaps are "two docs disagree" rather than "no doc covers it":** the staking menu (STAKE vs ARCH config), the underdog-tie rule (DATA/EDGE prose vs ARCH config), the `B`-selection rule (ruin-MC specified vs CI-bootstrap hand-waved). These are cheap to close and should be reconciled before Phase 0 freezes `config/`, because the multiple-testing family `K` and the DSR inputs are computed from the config, so a config/prose mismatch silently corrupts the significance correction.

3. **The honest-prior framing is a strength, not a gap.** The plan's pre-commitment to report `λ*=0` / wide-CI-around-zero (A.2.4) and the demotion of 2026 to descriptive are exactly right; the register's role is to ensure that when the verdict is delivered, the *cost* and *capacity* numbers behind it are real rather than idealized, so "do not bet" is defended on net-of-cost, executable terms.
